Blueberry, Pomegranate
and Hemp Seed Smoothie

Ingredients

1 Serving

Save

Print

Save to Pinterest

Cooking time: 5 min

1 large banana

1 cup blueberries

2 tbsps hemp seeds

1 medium pomegranate (juiced)

Recipe by:

Nina Rodrigues

November 24, 2017

Follow me on:

METHOD

  • 1

    Start by squeezeing the juice from the pomegranate (or add the whole seeds).

  • 2

    Combine all the ingredients in a blender and blend them.

  • 3

    Add as much water and ice as you like.

  • 4

    Enjoy!
